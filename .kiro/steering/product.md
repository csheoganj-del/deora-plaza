# Product Overview

## DEORA Plaza - Restaurant Management System

DEORA Plaza is a comprehensive restaurant and hospitality management platform built for multi-unit operations. The system manages restaurants, cafes, bars, hotels, and garden venues with integrated ordering, billing, inventory, and customer management.

### Core Business Units
- **Restaurant**: Full-service dining with waiter management
- **Cafe**: Quick-service operations with takeaway support
- **Bar**: Beverage service with specialized menu and measurements
- **Hotel**: Room bookings, guest management, and room service
- **Garden**: Event space bookings and catering

### Key Features
- **Multi-department Operations**: Unified system across all business units
- **Order Management**: Complete order flow from placement to settlement
- **Billing & GST**: Comprehensive billing with GST compliance and reporting
- **Customer Management**: Guest profiles, loyalty programs, and booking history
- **Inventory Management**: Stock tracking across all departments
- **Staff Management**: Role-based access and cross-unit staffing
- **Real-time Notifications**: Kitchen displays, waiter alerts, manager dashboards
- **Settlement & Reporting**: Daily reconciliation and financial reporting
- **Offline Support**: Queue-based operations for network interruptions

### Target Users
- Restaurant owners and managers
- Hotel operators
- Multi-unit hospitality businesses
- Staff (waiters, kitchen, front desk, managers)
- Customers (via QR ordering and booking systems)

The system emphasizes zero-downtime operations with redundant backend architecture and comprehensive audit trails for compliance and business intelligence.
